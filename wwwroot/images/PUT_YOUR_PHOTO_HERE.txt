Put your photo here and name it "profile.jpg".

The Home page (Components/Pages/Home.razor) looks for it at:
wwwroot/images/profile.jpg

Recommended: a square photo, at least 500x500px, well-lit, plain background.
If you'd rather use a .png, change PersonalInfo.PhotoUrl in Data/PortfolioData.cs
to "images/profile.png".

Until a photo is added here, the homepage automatically shows a circle
with your initials instead -- so it's safe to leave this empty for now.

You can delete this file once your photo is in place.
